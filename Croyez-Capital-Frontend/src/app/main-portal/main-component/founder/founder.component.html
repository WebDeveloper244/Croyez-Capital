PARTH SHAH

Parth Shah exemplifies the essence of a passionate and outcome-oriented entrepreneur. Currently at the helm of Croyez Management and as the CEO, he embarked on his entrepreneurial journey immediately after graduating from George Brown College, Toronto, at the age of 19. Parth's unwavering vision was to create a comprehensive solution that caters to every requirement of an online brand or business.

Parth has fostered the inception and growth of numerous e-commerce brands and businesses, propelling them beyond the thresholds of 7 and 8-figure revenues within 12-36 months. His commitment to customer-centric methodologies, intertwined with an emphasis on brand loyalty, has been instrumental in writing remarkable success stories of Croyez Management's esteemed clientele. In the process, Parth has assembled a team of industry-leading talents who possess an insatiable appetite for conquering novel challenges.

Parth's second venture operates as an innovative powerhouse that facilitates the growth and expansion of artists, brands, and businesses in the web3 realm. With a diverse repertoire that spans from minting/marketing NFT collections to managing web3 portfolios by strategically investing in projects boasting robust foundations at the nascent stages, delivers unparalleled solutions that propel its clients toward unmitigated success in the web3 space.

Beyond his professional commitments, Parth remains resolute in fulfilling his social responsibilities through his philanthropic venture, Better Together - Ahmedabad. Through this notable NGO, Parth ensures the sustenance of over 320 underprivileged children in India by providing them with daily meals, empowering them to focus on their studies and secure a promising future for themselves and their families alike.
